So... what is this about?

Hello! My name is Sofia Moore, a student from St. Catherine’s Moorlands School here in Buenos Aires. I’m on Level 5, and this year it was our time for the Personal Project, part of the MYP. I decided to explore this topic because I’ve always been interested in reading, especially nowadays, and I think it would be interesting to try and show my classmates what reading is about and how they could start. In terms of the product itself, I created a website because I really like coding and programming, and I do pretty well at school in this subject, so I thought why not? This project is not completed, of course, because it would take me too long to finish completely; however, I made almost all of the main functions that it should have, along with some examples, so I think it will be easy for people to understand what I’m trying to communicate. Hope you like it!

Note: books that have an "*" are those of which I have read!
